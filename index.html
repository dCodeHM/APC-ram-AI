Course Registration

Streamline your academic journey with our registration process

Registration Process

  • 1. Go to rams.apc.edu.ph

    2. Enter your School E-mail and password

    3. Click "Login" to access your student dashboard

  • 1. Navigate to "Course Registration" section using the side panel

    2. In the "Registrar Transaction" section, select "Registration"

    3. Click "Confirm Registration" to proceed

    4. You can also add or drop a subject by clicking the "Add/Remove Subject" button

    5. Confirm your Registration

  • 1. After confirming your registration, select "Assessment" section and view the subject fees

    2. You can proceed to the payment section by clicking the "Online Payment" button in the side panel section

    3. Or you can go to the Finance office in the 4th floor to pay your fees

    4. You will need the following documents to pay your fees

    • School ID
    • Payment
    • Registration Receipt

    5. After paying your fees, you will receive a registration receipt and go to the Registrar office to get your class schedule approved.
